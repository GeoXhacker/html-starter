Our Mission

To regenerate East Africa's forests while creating sustainable investment opportunities and improving local communities' livelihoods.

Our Story

From Seed to Forest: The EcoInvest Journey

EcoInvest was founded in 2025 by four individuals Itaaga, Jonathan, Wandera, and Yakub who shared a vision of combining sustainable reforestation with impact investment in East Africa.

What began as a small project in Uganda is planning to expand across the region, with tree planting initiatives in Tanzania, Kenya, and Rwanda. Our approach centers on indigenous species that support local ecosystems while providing long-term value.

Our Values

The principles that guide our work and define our commitment to sustainable forestry and ethical investment.

Environmental Stewardship

We prioritize native species, biodiversity, and ecosystem health in all our forestry projects, ensuring sustainable land management for generations to come.

Community Partnership

We collaborate with local communities, respecting traditional knowledge, creating employment opportunities, and ensuring fair benefit sharing from our projects.

Transparency & Integrity

We maintain open communication with our investors, providing regular updates, impact reports, and clear financial reporting to build lasting trust.
